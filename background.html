Experience

I am an experienced mathematics tutor specialising in GCSE (both Foundation and Higher tiers) and A-Level, helping students to get to grips with content and prepare for exams. I have supported students with a wide range of needs, from getting over the pass/fail borderline at GCSE to securing an A* at A Level.

 

I have taught Mathematics and Statistics in Higher Education for over 10 years. I was an Associate Professor of Mathematics Education at University of Nottingham, where I led on curriculum development and student experience. I am now the Mathematics and Statistics Support Lead in the Centre for Academic Achievement at the University of Leicester.

 

My experience teaching in University equips me to help students prepare for University Entrance Exams (TMUA, STEP). I can also offer advice and coaching to students intending to go to University, particularly for Mathematics and related courses.

 

I have a current Enhanced DBS certificate.
