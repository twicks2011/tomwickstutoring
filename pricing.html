Availability

I tutor in-person or online.

 

I have extremely limited availability for the 2025-26 academic year.

 

I am currently taking bookings for TMUA tuition, to start in June, July or August 2026.

 

I am also taking bookings for GCSE and A Level tuition to start in September 2026.

 

I usually do tutorials between 5pm and 8pm on weekdays. If it proves impossible to find a convenient time during the week then I may be able to offer tuition at weekends, although this is unusual.

 

I live in Cotgrave, Nottinghamshire. In-person tuition would normally take place at your home, provided you live within 1-hour of Cotgrave. Online tuition usually takes place using Teams.
